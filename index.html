A Visual Analytics System for Evaluating Public Safety Personnel

In this project, we present you with a series of visualizations to represent various statistics related to public safety organizations across the four major cities of Boston, Chicago, New York, San Francisco. We analyze the performance of the public safety officers based on the severity score of the type of offences they have dealt with. The severity of score of the offenses have been calculated according to the survey completed by the students of this class.
